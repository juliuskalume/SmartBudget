Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SmartBudget ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our financial management application.

Key Points:
  • Your financial data is encrypted and stored securely
  • SMS data is processed locally on your device when possible
  • We never sell your personal or financial data
  • You have control over data sharing and cloud synchronization

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password, country, and preferred currency
  • Profile Information: Name, avatar image URL (optional)
  • Financial Data: Manual transaction entries, budget goals, savings targets

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, error logs
  • SMS Data (Android only): Bank alert messages for transaction import

1.3 Third-Party Data

  • Currency Exchange Rates: Real-time rates from financial data providers
  • Authentication: OAuth tokens for cloud synchronization (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Functionality

  • Process and display your financial transactions
  • Generate financial analysis and insights
  • Calculate budgets and savings goals
  • Provide currency conversion services

2.2 SMS Processing (Android)

  • Scan incoming SMS messages for bank alerts
  • Extract transaction details (amount, date, merchant)
  • Automatically categorize and add transactions to your ledger
  • Store only confirmed transaction data, not raw SMS content

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Debug and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

2.4 Communication

  • Send important service updates and security notifications
  • Respond to support requests and bug reports
  • Provide customer service assistance

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device using encrypted databases. This includes:

  • Transaction history and categories
  • Budget settings and goals
  • User preferences and settings

3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

When you enable cloud synchronization:

  • Data is encrypted before transmission
  • Stored on secure, SOC 2 compliant servers
  • Protected by industry-standard encryption (AES-256)
  • Accessible only with your account credentials

3.3 SMS Data Handling

SMS Privacy Protection:
  • SMS messages are scanned locally on your device
  • Only transaction-relevant data is extracted and stored
  • Raw SMS content is never permanently stored or transmitted
  • You can disable SMS access at any time in device settings

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate SmartBudget:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Cloud database and authentication Encrypted user data (optional)
Firebase Analytics and crash reporting Anonymized usage data
Currency APIs Exchange rate data No personal data

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, including:

  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Protection against fraud or security threats
  • Response to government requests with proper legal process

5. Your Rights and Controls

5.1 Data Access and Portability

  • Export your transaction data in standard formats
  • Access all stored personal information
  • Request data portability between services

5.2 Data Correction and Deletion

  • Edit or correct your personal information
  • Delete individual transactions or entire account
  • Request complete data deletion from our systems

5.3 Privacy Controls

  • Disable SMS access in device settings
  • Opt out of cloud synchronization
  • Control data sharing preferences
  • Manage notification settings

6. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Transaction Data Until account deletion Service functionality
Account Information 3 years after account deletion Legal compliance
Usage Analytics 2 years Service improvement
SMS Content Never stored permanently Privacy protection

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Industry-standard encryption

8. Children's Privacy

SmartBudget is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • In-app notifications
  • Posting the updated policy in the app

Continued use of SmartBudget after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

SmartBudget Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartBudget collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app, including account features, SMS-based transaction import, AI assistance, cloud sync, and Smart Save+ simulations.

1) Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to users of SmartBudget web and Android experiences, including demo mode and authenticated cloud mode.

2) Information we process

Account and profile information

  • Email address and password (for cloud login).
  • Profile details such as name, avatar URL, country, and local currency preference.

Financial and app data

  • Transactions you import or enter manually (merchant, amount, category, date, debit/credit).
  • Savings and budgeting metrics generated from your entries (e.g., cash flow, trends, health score).
  • Smart Save+ data, such as protected holdings and simulated buy/sell records.

SMS-related data (Android permission flow)

  • When permission is granted, SmartBudget reads relevant SMS messages that appear financial in nature.
  • Only extracted transaction details needed for budgeting are saved to your ledger/account.
  • In supported flows, non-financial message content is not retained as budget entries.

AI and market insight inputs

  • Selected financial summary values may be sent to app APIs to generate categorization/advice and market insight cards.
  • Outputs may include recommendations, what-if scenarios, and explanatory text.

3) How we use information

  • Provide core budgeting, visualization, and analytics features.
  • Generate AI-enhanced categorization, advice cards, and scenario insights.
  • Enable cloud sync and account security operations (login, profile updates, password updates).
  • Operate Smart Save+ simulations and protect app integrity and reliability.

4) Legal bases and permissions

  • Consent: for optional permissions such as Android SMS access.
  • Contract/performance: to provide services requested by you inside the app.
  • Legitimate interests: for app security, diagnostics, and feature quality.

5) Storage, retention, and security

  • Demo mode data may remain only on your device/browser session.
  • Cloud mode data is stored in backend services used by SmartBudget (for example, account and synced ledger state).
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no method of transmission/storage is 100% secure.
  • Data is retained while your account remains active, unless earlier deletion is requested or required by law.

6) Sharing and third-party services

SmartBudget may use service providers to operate app functionality, including authentication, storage, and AI/API processing.

  • Data is shared only as needed to provide features and maintain service quality.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

7) Your choices and rights

  • You can edit profile details from the Profile screen.
  • You can revoke SMS permission at the device level.
  • You can request account deletion from Account Actions in the Profile screen.
  • You can contact support for privacy-related questions or requests.

8) Children’s privacy

SmartBudget is not intended for young children. If you believe a child has provided personal data without appropriate authorization, contact support so we can investigate and remove data where applicable.

9) International use

SmartBudget may be accessed from multiple regions. By using the app, you understand your data may be processed in locations where service providers operate.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material updates will be reflected by revising the date at the top of this page.
